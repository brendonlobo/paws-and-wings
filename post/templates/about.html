About

Hi,

This is an initiative taken for our four-legged friends and other pet friends! This idea is my brainchild created during the Lockdown of 2020. When I noticed as we humans struggled to make ends meet in that tough time , we were not alone! Our pet friends have struggled a lot in this time period.Many people who migrated to various other places had to do arrangements for their pets , which was a hectic task..

So this website is a medium where people could share the lifestyle of their pets on the 'Master Of networks' - The Internet! People could share images and funny videos of their pets. As well as some grooming tips of their pet kid. Also they could post if they need someone to take care of their pets (temporarily/permanentaly), they could come in touch with other users using the chat facility. People could also find interested people to adopt the offsprings of their pets.

Note:

This site is also intended for people to share if any animal is found in distress and the councerned authorities will be helping the needy. Afterall the site is created because every life matters!
